Welcome to Dad Chat!

Dad Chat is an app that can be used to find other single fathers and arrange play-dates. Check out what our site has to offer:

Find Places Near You

DadChat offers searching for daycares, lawyers, and doctors reviewed by other fathers. Just click the drop down at the top of the page and click find. You will then be prompted for your location to get the best accuracy near you. Alternatively, you can input a zip code to search.

See Upcoming Events

Head over to the events page to see events and playdates organized by other fathers. You can also see the most upcoming events in the right sidebar of this page.

Message other Fathers

Once you have found a event you are interested in you can message the organizer via our messages tab. Its a great place to know more about events, make friends, and learn tips from other fathers.
